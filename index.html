Welcome to your Subscription Access System

The Subscription Access System is a simple and user-friendly platform that allows users to register, log in, and access content based on their subscription plans. The system consists of four main pages
1. The Home Page, which provides an overview and navigation links
2. The Registration Page, where users can sign up with their details
3. The Login Page, which authenticates users and grants access to their accounts
4. The Dashboard Page, where users can view their current subscription plan, upgrade it, and access exclusive content.
